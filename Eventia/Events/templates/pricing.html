Frequently Asked Questions

Eventzilla is free if your event is free for your attendees. This works for most simple events like gatherings and meetups. For additional features like customizing the event page to match your brand, premium bundles are available for a flat fee.

There are no upfront costs for basic usage. Subscription fees apply only if you choose advanced plans or premium features.

Payment processing fees depend on the payment provider and typically range from 2% to 4% of the transaction value.

Remittances are processed within 3-5 business days after the event is concluded, depending on your payment provider.
